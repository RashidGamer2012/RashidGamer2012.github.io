Our Mission

This society is established with the aim of promoting awareness and providing psychological support sessions for youth experiencing bullying and harassment. We strongly advocate for online integrity, ensuring that everyone feels safe, respected, and free to express themselves. In addition to supporting victims of online abuse, we strive to resolve conflicts through positive and constructive approaches, offering victims a safe place where they can heal and be heard. Our ultimate goal is to build a more empathetic, informed, and mentally resilient student community through awareness sessions, open discussions, and peer support initiatives..
